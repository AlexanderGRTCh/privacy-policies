Privacy Policy

App: Habit Tracker 2026

Habit Tracker 2026 ("the App") helps users track personal goals with daily check-ins, streaks, and reminders.

No Cloud Storage

All goal and habit data is stored locally on your device only. The App does not require account creation and does not transmit personal data to our servers.

Data You Provide

  • Goal information you enter (such as goal titles, descriptions, dates, and reminder text).

This data is stored locally on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Goals, check-ins, missed days, and settings are stored locally using on-device storage (Hive / SharedPreferences).

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (for ads)
  • Google Play Billing (for subscription processing)
  • RevenueCat (for subscription management and receipt validation)

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies, including device identifiers, advertising ID, non-precise location (country/region level), ad interaction analytics, and anonymous subscription status.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from apps that use AdMob
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

How Data Is Used

  • To provide core app functionality (goal tracking, reminders, check-ins).
  • To display ads (if enabled) and measure ad performance.
  • To process and restore subscriptions.

Data Sharing

  • The App does not sell personal data.
  • Data may be shared with third-party providers (such as Google and RevenueCat) only as required to provide ads and subscriptions.

Your Choices

  • You can delete goals inside the App to remove locally stored data.
  • You can uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • You can purchase Premium to remove all ads.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the email below so we can take appropriate action.
